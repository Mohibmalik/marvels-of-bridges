Bridges work by spanning a body of water or other obstacle and providing a safe way for people and vehicles to cross.

There are many different types of bridges, including suspension bridges, arch bridges, beam bridges, and cable-stayed bridges.

Bridges are built using a variety of materials, depending on the type of bridge and the purpose it will serve. Common materials used in bridge construction include concrete, steel, and wood.
